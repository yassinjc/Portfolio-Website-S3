OFFERING BEAUTY IN THE BEST WAY

For my passion project in semester 3, i decided to make a website for the hair & make-up artist Rayshree. The purpose of this website is to showcase all of the beauty services offered by Rayshree with all the needed information, along with an overview of all of her previous work in a clear way.

After having done enough research and having spoken with Rayshree on multiple occasions to set a foundation for the website, i started working on the design. We went back and forth reviewing different designs until we eventually had a finalised design. After usertesting this design i started developing the website to make it fully functional and ready for Rayshree to be used.
