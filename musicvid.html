VISUALISING MUSIC

Besides studying IT, i also spend a lot of my time on making music. For my Sprint X project in semester 2, i decided to combine my hobby with my school which lead to me editing a full music video for one of my songs. With the help of my friends we shot the music video, after which i edited all the footage into one complete music video.

We used 2 different cameras for shooting the music video. A Nikon D780 Camera and an iPhone. For the iPhone we used an app called Rarevision VHS, this gave the footage an old VHS-look which i thought would fit really nice in the music video. For the editing proces i used the programs Adobe Premiere Pro in combination with Adobe After Effects. My goal with this project was to create a fast-paced, energetic music video with various special effects to create a nice flow.
