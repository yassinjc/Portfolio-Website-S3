DISPLAYING MY SPECIALITIES

In semester 2 i made a portfolio website in which i displayed all of my progress in completing the learning outcomes. I wanted this portfolio website to have a more industrial type of look as i felt like that style expressed myself in the best way possible. A handful of designs eventually lead to this finalised portfolio website.

I started with making a couple of designs in Adobe XD. The goal was to experiment with certain styles in which i could eventually decide a final design style which fit me the most. Eventually i came up with an industrial look that i really liked. After making certain changes in the design, based on feedback of my teachers, the design was finalised. I then developed the design into a fully functional website and the end result was exactly what i wanted.
